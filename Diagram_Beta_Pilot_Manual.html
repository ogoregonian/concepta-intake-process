Beta Pilot — Manual Workflow & Tooling

The first pilot workflow we follow. Manual process — no AI Agent yet. Eight stages from request capture through approval and carve-out, covering both new requests and reported issues. Differences between the two are called out inline at the stages where the flow diverges. Click any step for the What, Who, and Why.

Beta Pilot · Phase 1 Once we iterate and validate this through the corporate site pilot, we move to the next phase. The Phase 1 innovation lives in stages 1–8 below; the post-approval delivery pipeline (Sprint Candidates → TO DO → IN DEVELOPMENT → ... → UAT APPROVED) runs as it does today.
