💡 Presentation Tips

1️⃣
Start with the Executive Brief — use sections 1 and 2 (Background & Goals) to frame the problem before showing any diagrams. Leadership needs the why before the how.
2️⃣
When walking Diagram 1 — emphasize that the AI Agent owns the handoff, not the PDM. The internal team confirms, not executes. This is the key operational shift.
3️⃣
On Diagrams 2 and 3 — point to the continuous monitoring block in the AI Agent lane. This is the lean, rule-based automation story that addresses the "foundation before optimization" action item.
4️⃣
Close with the Billing View as the answer to Raul's client visibility ask. Frame it as a draft direction — the structure is right, the data will come from Zoho once the process is live.
5️⃣
If asked about the AI Agent build — "Our internal labs team owns it. We're starting lean — rule-based triggers and automations — and expanding as we build confidence."
